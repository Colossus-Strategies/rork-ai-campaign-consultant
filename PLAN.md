# Build a Colossus marketing website matching the app

A polished marketing website for Colossus — the AI Campaign Consultant — designed to drive App Store downloads and service inquiries. It will mirror the app's "campaign war room" look: deep charcoal/navy backgrounds with burnished gold accents.

## Design
- Dark, premium aesthetic: near-black and deep navy backgrounds with molten gold accents, just like the app.
- Cinematic hero with the gold-arc background art already in your project, large serif headline "Built For Your Race".
- Smooth scroll animations, subtle motion on cards, and gold glow on buttons.
- Fully responsive for phone, tablet, and desktop.

## Pages / Sections (single landing page)
- **Hero**: Headline, short pitch, "Download on the App Store" button, and a phone mockup feel.
- **The Consultant**: Highlights the 24/7 race-aware AI chat coach.
- **The Six Modules**: A grid showing Fundraising, Voter Contact, Messaging, Field Operations, Compliance, and Earned Media with their taglines.
- **District Intelligence**: Showcase of custom voter-file and precinct data pulls.
- **Pro Services**: Cards for Colossus Fundraising Pro and Deep Strategy.
- **How it works / Why Colossus**: Trust-building section ("Educate · Empower · Win").
- **Contact / Get Started**: A simple inquiry form for campaigns that want services, plus a final call-to-action.
- **Footer**: Branding, tagline, and links.

## Notes
- The site is a marketing/landing presence — it points people to the iOS app rather than reproducing the full logged-in app.
- I'll use placeholder text for the App Store link and contact email, which you can give me to finalize.
