# TestFlight — What to Test (v1.1.1)

Thanks for testing! New in this build:

## District Tab (NEW)
1. Open the **District** tab (map icon, 4th tab).
2. First time only: read and accept the **voter data use** modal.
3. **Dashboard** — confirm you see your district summary, party donut, status breakdown, turnout bars, and top 5 precincts. Pull to refresh.
4. Check the **LAST REFRESH** badge and tap the **LAST INGEST** pill to see ingest status per county.

## Voter Universe
5. Switch to **Voters** — try filters (party, status, precinct, age range, turnout score).
6. Tap any row to see vote history. Try **Export CSV**.

## Targeting Lists
7. Switch to **Lists** → **Build a list**. Try each preset:
   - Door-knock walk list
   - Phone bank list
   - Persuasion universe
   - Custom
8. Confirm map view renders and CSV export works.

## AI Consultant Tools
9. Open the AI Consultant and ask:
   - "Summarize my district."
   - "Where should I door-knock first?"
   - "What's my turnout looking like in the last four generals?"
   The consultant should respond with **real numbers** for your race, not generic advice.

## Research Mode
10. Toggle the **building.columns** icon in chat. The strip should read **RESEARCH · FEC + BALLOTPEDIA**. Ask "Who are the top donors to [opponent]?" — answers should cite fec.gov / ballotpedia.org URLs.

## Report bugs to
support@colossusstrategies.com — include screenshots and the race you're testing as.
